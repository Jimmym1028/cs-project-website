Computer Science and Video Game Development

Course: Intro to Computer Science

Topic: How computer science is used in video games

Abstract: This website gives a simple overview of how ideas from computer science help create video games. First, we look at the history of video games and how early computers were used for games. Then we talk about how modern games use programming, graphics, and networks. Finally, we look at how future games might use new computer science topics like artificial intelligence and virtual reality.
